Privacy Policy for Compose: AI Email Draft

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

Compose: AI Email Draft ("Compose") is designed with privacy as a core principle. All AI-powered draft generation happens entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Compose does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, analytics, or diagnostics to any server.

On-Device Processing

All email drafts are generated using Apple's on-device Foundation Models (Apple Intelligence). Your messages, prompts, and generated drafts never leave your device.

Local Storage

Draft history is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data is not synced, backed up to any external service, or accessible to anyone other than you. You can delete your draft history at any time from within the app.

Third-Party Services

Compose does not use any third-party services, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or external APIs.

Tracking

Compose does not track you in any way. We do not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track.

Children's Privacy

Compose does not collect any data from any user, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
