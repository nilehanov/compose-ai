Compose is a private AI email and message drafter that runs entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence. No accounts, no cloud, no data collection — just polished email drafts in seconds.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Choose a template: Reply, New Email, Follow-Up, Decline, or Complaint
2. Paste the original message or describe what you want to write
3. Pick your tone: Formal, Friendly, Assertive, Diplomatic, or Apologetic
4. Tap Generate — Apple Intelligence crafts a polished draft on-device
5. Copy or share your draft

5 PROFESSIONAL TEMPLATES
- Reply to Email: Craft the perfect response to any message
- New Email: Write a brand-new email from a simple description
- Follow-Up: Send a polite nudge without sounding pushy
- Decline / Reject: Turn down requests gracefully and professionally
- Complaint: File a professional complaint that gets results

5 TONE OPTIONS
Choose Formal for business, Friendly for colleagues, Assertive for urgency, Diplomatic for sensitive topics, or Apologetic when you need to make amends.

COMPLETELY PRIVATE
- All AI processing happens on-device with Apple Intelligence
- No data is collected, stored, or sent to any server
- No accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking
- Your messages never leave your device

DRAFT HISTORY
Every generated draft is saved locally so you can revisit, copy, and reuse your best emails anytime.

Requires iPhone or iPad with Apple Intelligence support running iOS 26 or later.
